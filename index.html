Stephen Souvall, DPT

Doctor of Physical Therapy. Founder and CEO of On The Go Physical Therapy

Stephen is owner and lead physical therapist at On The Go Physical Therapy. He has extensive experience in the diagnosis and treatment of musculoskeletal injuries, pre- and post-operative care, sports rehab, work injury rehabilitation, and auto accident rehabilitation. Stephen is passionate about helping his patients achieve their goals, whether they hope to get back to work, return to skiing, be able to garden again, or hold their grandchildren without pain. He uses an evidence-based, individualized treatment approach to treat his patients, with the goal that they finish PT the strongest and most capable they have ever been.

Stephen is a native of Salt Lake City and received his Physical Therapy degree from the University of Puget Sound in Tacoma, WA. When he isn’t treating patients, Stephen enjoys spending time with his family, mountain biking, skiing, hiking, running, and rock climbing.
